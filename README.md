Developer. Teacher. Dreamer.
----------------------------

Started my career in the "Myspace" era, curating my page so that my customized pointer effortlessly sparkled across the page while "Lady in the Blue Dress" by Senses Fail automatically played in the background. That was my first taste of HTML, since then my career has had a winding path. I became STRATA IT certified because my high school had a program that allowed me to refurbish and keep my first laptop, that would carry me through sophomore year of college. I studied Computer Science and Applied Mathematics and earned my B.S. in Comp Sci in May of 2020. Since then I have dabbled in many technologies, languages, and software. I originally learned to code in C++ & Java, currently my language of choice is JavaScript. Graduating during the middle of a global pandemic led to interesting career opportunities, such as working with one of my now closest friends at his Technical Consulting Agency to educating the youth of NYC Public Schools, where the children had been one of biggest inspirations. Though I have fulfilled many roles since college, I am looking forward to leveraging solid skills in collaboration, communication, and development as a programmer while utilizing specialties in math, logic, and cross-platform coding.
